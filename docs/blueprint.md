# **App Name**: Fresh Fades

## Core Features:

- Appointment Booking: Allow clients to view available time slots and book appointments for services like Weekly Lineup ($25), Fresh Fades ($40), Cut & Shave ($75), and Shave ($40).
- Appointment Management: Admin panel for the barber to view, approve, reschedule, or cancel appointments.
- Client Profile Management: Enable barber to manage client profiles, including contact information and appointment history.
- Service and Pricing Display: Display service details ("Bi-Weekly Linups" - $40/month Subscription, "Weekly Lineups" - $75/m subscription, "Unlimited Linups"  $145/month subscription. "Bi-Weekly Fade" $65/monthly subscription, "Weekly Fade" $120/month saubscription, "Unlimited Fades" $160/month subscription, "Fresh Fade" $35, "Cut & Shave" $30, "Fresh Fade, Cut & Shave" $60) and their respective costs. All subscriptions come with 2 months free when paid annually.
- Loyalty Program: Include a loyalty program, every 10 paid appointments should give the customer a Free haircut with up to a $50 value

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Dark gray (#333) for a modern, professional look.
- Secondary color: Light gray (#f0f0f0) for backgrounds and subtle contrasts.
- Accent: Red (#710) for buttons and highlights, conveying a sense of freshness and cleanliness.
- Clean and modern typography for readability.
- Use sharp, minimalist icons for services and actions.
- Clean and structured layout for easy navigation.
